Booking assistant

A simple mock of the conversational flow customers will use to find a slot.

Hi! I can help you book an appointment. What day works best for you?
This Friday.
Great. I see available slots at 10:00 AM, 11:30 AM, and 2:00 PM. Which one would you like?
11:30 AM, please.
Done. I’ve requested an 11:30 AM Classic Haircut for this Friday. You’ll see it in your bookings once the owner approves.
